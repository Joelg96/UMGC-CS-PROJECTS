Tears of Mars, an inspiring upcoming band, has left an indelible mark on the airwaves and listeners. Raised in the suburban town of Joppa just on the outskirts of Baltimore, MD, They are blazing their way along the East Coast. The band consists of five brothers

Members:

  1. Josiah Orsie - Vocals, Guitar, Keyboards
  2. Luke Orsie - Lead guitar
  3. Micah Orsie - Bass
  4. Elias Orsie - Drums
  5. Judah Orsie - Keyboards, Percussion, Ambience
